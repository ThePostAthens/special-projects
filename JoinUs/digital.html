DIGITAL

The digital side of The Post is made up of design, photography, multimedia and social media. These staffs help visualize and promote content published through each editorial staff. No experience is necessary to join any of these staffs, as you will be trained by each editor.

  • PHOTO

    Photographers cover stories for every staff at The Post — ranging from breaking news, features and events on campus and in Athens.

    Editors:
    Kelsey Boeing & Emilee Chinn

  • DESIGN

    Designers can create illustrations and graphics to supplement stories as well as design the weekly tabloid and use UX/UI design and web development for online special projects.

    Directors:
    Riley Scott, Rilee Lockhart, Midge Mazur

  • MULTIMEDIA

    The multimedia team creates and edits videos each week and work closely with each editorial staff. They also help edit podcasts that come in each week.

    Director:
    Nicholas Langer, Faith Lucas

  • SOCIAL MEDIA

    Staff members will help our online presence and develop strategies to increase engagement on all The Post’s social media feeds.

    Director:
    Matthew Geiger
