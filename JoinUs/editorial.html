EDITORIAL

The editorial side of The Post is made up of five sections: news, culture, sports, opinion and The Beat. Our copy staff copy edits all work from each section.

Writers are encouraged to explore different topics to find out what they are most interested and passionate to write about. Ideas are encouraged to be pitched to a section editor or writers can be given a story by their editor. Writer positions are unpaid but is still a good experience for clips and feedback on writing skills.

  • NEWS

    Reporter have the opportunity to cover important issues on campus or locally. Topics include breaking news, politics, education, cops/crime and investigations.

    Editors:
    Abby Miller, Nolan Simmons & Ian McKenzie

  • SPORTS

    The sports staff covers all Ohio University sports teams and has access to players and coaches whether it be through game coverage, features/profiles and more.

    Editors:
    Anthony Poisal & Matt Parker

  • CULTURE

    Culture writers cover all things creative in the area, whether it be theater productions, the local music scene, concerts on campus and more.

    Editors:
    Baylee DeMuth & Riley Runnells

  • OPINION

    Columnists write regular columns, usually on a weekly or bi-weekly basis, that express views on important issues on campus and in Athens.

    Editor:
    Shelby Campbell

  • THE BEAT

    This staff produces blogs, listicles, TV/movie reviews, album reviews and also has its own daily newsletter, Post Haste.

    Editors:
    Molly Schramm & Mady Lewellyn

  • COPY

    Some members have started out at The Post through joining the copy staff first. You don't have to know anything about AP style to get started.

    Copy Chief:
    Bre Offenberger
