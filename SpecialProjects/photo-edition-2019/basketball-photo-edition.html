Ohio guard Amani Burke drives to the basket against a defender during the Bobcats’ nonconference win over Coppin State on Dec. 8. Ohio finished its nonconference slate undefeated and broke the program’s single-season wins record on its way to a WNIT Quarterfinal appearance.



Hannah Ruhoff | PHOTO EDITOR

Former Ohio coach Saul Phillips coaches the Bobcats during a home game. Phillips’ contract wasn’t renewed after the season, and he finished his tenure in Athens with an 81-77 overall record, but he was just 40-50 in conference play. Phillips was let go on March 13 after the Bobcats were bounced from the MAC Tournament in the first round.



Anthony Warner | FOR THE POST

Ohio sophomore forward Jason Carter attacks the rim while Kent State defenders bear down on him during the Bobcats’ 66-52 loss in The Convo on Jan. 15. The Bobcats finished the season 14-17 and replaced coach Saul Phillips at the end of the season. Carter averaged 16.5 points per game, but chose to transfer to Xavier following the season.
