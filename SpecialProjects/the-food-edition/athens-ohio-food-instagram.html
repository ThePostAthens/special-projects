The best foodstagrams of Athens you should be following

Taylor Johnston / Digital Production Editor

Some local eateries in Athens take pride in showing off their dishes on social media.

If you enjoy being graced with beautiful food every time you log into your Instagram, then check out some some of these locations that have some of the best instagram accounts:

Fluff

A post shared by The Fluff Athens (@fluffbakeryathens) on Feb 21, 2018 at 5:59am PST

The bakery and cafe, located at 8 N Court St., posts pictures of delicious pies, cakes and other pastries.

Casa Nueva

A post shared by Casa Nueva (@casanuevaathens) on Mar 22, 2018 at 2:06pm PDT

A Mexican-inspired eatery, located at 6 W. State St., which posts pictures of lunch/dinner specials and new menu items on a regular basis.

Thai Paradise

A post shared by Thai Paradise (@thaiparadiseohio) on Nov 1, 2017 at 12:19pm PDT

Thai Paradise, 102 W. Union St., brings authentic cuisine to Athens by providing a variety of Asian culture and ambiance.

Stephen's On Court

A post shared by Stephens On Court (@stephensoncourt) on Mar 29, 2018 at 11:27am PDT

A casual eatery, 66 N. Court St., that serves comfort foods such as burgers, pasta and more which can be accompanied by one of their cocktails.

Court Street Diner

A post shared by Court Street Diner (@thecourtstreetdiner) on Feb 12, 2018 at 8:15pm PST

Grab breakfast, lunch, dinner or even a milkshake at this retro Athens diner, 18 N. Court St., which recently upgraded to a new menu.

Jackie O's Pub and Brewery

A post shared by Jackie O's Pub and Brewery (@jackieosbrewery) on Dec 22, 2017 at 9:04am PST

Find a hand-crafted beer to enjoy by checking out this local brewery’s Instagram. The company has locations at 22 W. Union St. and 25 Campbell St.

Donkey Coffee and Espresso

A post shared by Donkey Coffee andEspresso (@donkeycoffeeohio) on Feb 3, 2018 at 4:13pm PST

Grab some fair trade coffee and a local pastry at this iconic Athens cafe, 17 W. Washington St.

Athens Farmers Market

A post shared by Athens Farmers Market (@athensohfarmersmarket) on Mar 13, 2018 at 7:43pm PDT

Every Saturday year-round and Wednesdays during the months of April through December, farmers and producers provide great options to choose from, whether it be hand-selected produce or baked goods. The market is located in the parking lot at 1000 E. State St.

Herbal Sage Tea and Tisane Co.

A post shared by Herbal Sage Tea & Tisane Co. (@herbalsagetea) on Feb 28, 2018 at 10:11am PST

A local herbalist provides handcrafted tea blends to the Athens community.

Brenen's Coffee Cafe

A post shared by Brenen's Coffee Cafe (@brenens_coffee_cafe) on Jul 5, 2017 at 6:17am PDT

Grab a sandwich, coffee or pastry at this Court Street cafe.
