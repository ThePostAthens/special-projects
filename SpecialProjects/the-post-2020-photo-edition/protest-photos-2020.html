Tanner Pearson | FOR THE POST

(From right to left) Tim Dransfield, Peggy Gish, Nancy Beres, and Jo Foley stand outside Athens County Courthouse, at the corner of Washington St. and Court St. on Monday, Jan. 27, 2020. The weekly vigil, held on Monday 11:30am to 12:30pm, has been in the same spot for 40 years.

Hannah Ruhoff | FOR THE POST

Students stand outside Cutler Hall on November 25, 2019 to protest Ohio University's budget crisis and proposed staff cuts.

Erin Burk | FOR THE POST

Garrett Vorisek (left), Wren Ballew (center), and Myles Ballew (right) pose with their bikes at the Global Climate Strike at the Village Bakery in Athens, Ohio on September 20, 2019.

Alec Syrvalin | FOR THE POST

Alex Armstrong and Jacob Chaffin lead the protest on Monday, November 25, 2019.

Nate Swanson | STAFF PHOTOGRAPHER

An enormous crowd of Ohio University students swarm far-right gun rights advocate Kaitlyn Bennett as she is guided through the campus by her security guard on Monday, Feb. 17, 2020. Bennett has gained popularity for her controversial "Liberty Hangout" show that she hosts out of Kent State University.

Nate Swanson | STAFF PHOTOGRAPHER

Gun rights advocate Kaitlyn Bennett is picked up by her security guard to move her through the swarm of students protesting her presence on Ohio University's campus.
