(From right to left) Tim Dransfield, Peggy Gish, Nancy Beres, and Jo Foley stand outside Athens County Courthouse, at the corner of Washington St. and Court St. on Monday, Jan. 27, 2020. The weekly vigil, held on Monday 11:30am to 12:30pm, has been in the same spot for 40 years.

Ohio University’s Jordan Earnest wrestles against Gardner-Webb’s Gabriel Pickett on Feb. 23, 2020 at The Convo. Ohio went on to win against Gardner-Webb 40-6.

Melody Spicer helps her son Tate Spicer (7) mix together a stir-fry at Nelsonville Public Library, on Feb. 26, 2020. FEAST is an 18-week workshop that meets Wednesdays at 5:30pm. The two frequent the workshop, learning to prepare healthy meals that are focused on a plant-based diet and bringing the meals into their own home.

Looking at Appalachia founder, Roger May, pictured in Schoonover Center 145, surrounded by photographs cultivated from the collection of online submissions, on Tuesday, Feb. 18, 2020. Looking at Appalachia is a collaborative photo project, including photographs taken anywhere in the 420 counties that make up the region.

Ohio forward Andrew Sacca (No. 18) flying down the ice against John Carroll on Friday, Sept. 27, 2019 at Bird Arena.

Ohio University forward Kyle Craddick (No.8) fights for the puck against Stony Brook defenseman Mike Kowicki (No. 23) and Stony Brook Goalie Sean Keating (No. 29). Ohio went on to win against Stony Brook 3-1.
