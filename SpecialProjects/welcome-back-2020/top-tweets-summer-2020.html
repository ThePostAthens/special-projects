Top Tweets of summer 2020

By Madyson Lewellyn | The Beat Editor

A fter being away from campus for nearly 5 months, it’s safe to say most people were getting homesick from Athens. The world nearly stopped for some time and most people filled their agenda-less days with hours of scrolling through Twitter or posting their feelings out of boredom. Nonetheless, there was no shortage of amazing (and sometimes cringey) Twitter content this summer and it’s time for a highlight reel. Here are the top tweets of summer 2020:

The ghosts of the Athens Lunatic Asylum took to Twitter to share their complaints throughout the summer:

why won’t any of the DoorDash drivers deliver to us
