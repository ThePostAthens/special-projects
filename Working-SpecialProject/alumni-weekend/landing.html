To The Post readers and Ohio Hockey fans,

Thank you for taking the time to get to this landing page. As a team of writers, designers and editors, we’ve worked diligently to present nothing but our best work to you, the readers.

Anthony and myself have covered the team extensively since the summer of 2017. In our short time covering the team, we’ve learned how important Ohio Hockey is to the city of Athens.

This landing page, designed by the talented Taylor Johnston, showcases some of our most challenging pieces yet. It is our hope that these stories serve their respective purposes to you.

As for myself, a couple stories maybe worth another glance:
1. Rex, the dinosaur
2. Last season on the ice
These stories are something I’m most proud of, and as a fan are something I’d want to read.

From Anthony:

Stay tuned for our weekend coverage of the 60th anniversary celebration.
